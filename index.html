Description

A password is a secret combination of letters, numbers, and symbols used to protect your personal information, online accounts, and digital devices. It acts like a key - only the person who knows the correct password can unlock access to the data or system.

Why Passwords Are Important

  1. Protect Personal Information - Keep your emails, social media, and banking accounts safe
  2. Prevent Unauthorized Access - Stop hackers from logging into your accounts
  3. Secure Online Transactions - Protect your payment information
  4. Maintain Privacy - Prevent others from reading your sensitive data

How to Create a Safe Password

  1. Use at least 8-12 characters or more
  2. Mix letters, numbers, and symbols
  3. Avoid personal information like your name or birthday
  4. Use different passwords for different accounts
  5. Change your passwords regularly
  6. Use a password manager to store passwords securely
